About Rare Beauty

Rare Beauty is a makeup-line which was founded by Selena Gomez, an American singer, actress, producer, and business woman. Rare Beauty is a makeup that is designed to make you feel amazing in it without masking what makes you unique because Rare Beauty itself is not about being someone else but being yourself.

Rare Beauty's Mission

We believe in the beauty of imperfections.

We nurture a caring, respectful community.

We create meaningful connections and relationships.

We champion authenticity and positivity.

We lead with transparency to build trust.

We believe there is power in being vulnerable.

Rare Beauty's History

In the beginning of Rare Beauty making which started all the way when Selena was still seven years old which was the starting of her career as a star. She has to spend hours on the makeup chair to find the perfect makeup look which sometimes ends up being just good. After years of struggling, Selena decided to launch Rare Beauty when she was 29 years old in September 2020. She knew she wanted to produce products that helped people improve rather than change their inherent traits.
